AR-ROB: iOS APPLICATION TO HELP HIGH SCHOOL STUDENT UNDERSTAND AEROBIC RESPIRATION

AR-Rob is an iOS application created to help high school students understand aerobic respiration. This application used augmented reality to visualize aerobic respiration.

Tech stack:

  • Swift
  • UIKit
  • ARKit
  • MVC design pattern
