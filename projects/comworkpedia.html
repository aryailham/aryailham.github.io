COMWORKPEDIA: MARKETPLACE FOR ARTWORK COMMISSION

Comworkpedia is a website that focused on artwork commission, and it is designed to be a place where an artist and an client can meet each other. With Comworkpedia, a client can search artworks that they want easily. And an artist can post their artwork commission service and get client from it. Comworkpedia provides six categories of artwork: Vector Art, WPAP (Wedha's Pop Art Portrait), Line Art, Low Poly Art, Flat Design, And Caricature.

Comworkpedia is a project that I and my team develop for Binus Festival 2020 which is held virtually at Bisanara Multivendor Platform (bisanara.com). My role is the Programmer for this project, and my responsibilities include: coding, debugging, testing, collaborate with team member, and design the ERD
