SABBY: iOS APPLICATION TO HELP HOBBYIST TO CONTROL IMPULSIVE BUYING

Sabby is an iOS application created to solve problems related to financial especially impulsive buying and hobby spending.

Sabby helps you to manage your savings and spendings towards your hobby-related items and encourages you to take some time to consider whether you really need to save up for it or not according to your budget capabilities. With Sabby, you can easily manage and list your desired items, add or cut budgets on your savings log each time you decided to buy something, and register other budgets you have aside from your dedicated hobby savings. When you have insufficient hobby savings, Sabby is ready to notify you to rethink before you finally decide to buy the item.

Tech stack:

  • Swift
  • UIKit
  • Core Data
  • CloudKit
  • Combine Framework
  • UserNotifications
  • MVVM design pattern
